What is Formula One?

Formula One is...

the highest class of international racing for open-wheel single-seater formula racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile. The FIA Formula One World Championship has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.


Formula One is...

the most popular motorsport in the world. It is known as one of the only racing series which does not need to refuel during races. It has a minimum age requirement and drivers require a SuperLicense, which ensures that they are the best of the best.

Formula one is...

a very complex motorsport. There are over 80,000 parts on each car. Teams spend up to $135 million Dollars per year on their cars. Their is staff consisting of engineers, drivers, team principals, and many other staff.
